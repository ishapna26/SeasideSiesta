ABOUT THE HOTEL

Welcome to The Seaside Siesta, a haven where the waves whisper and the sunset holds a promise of a new dawn. Nestled on the serene shores of a breathtaking coastal stretch, our hotel offers a gateway to a world of tranquility and picturesque beauty, designed exclusively for those who seek a perfect blend of comfort, elegance, and a touch of the whimsical. The vision of The Seaside Siesta emerged from a desire to create a sanctuary that not only stands out in luxury and service but also weaves itself into the fabric of coastal charm and hospitality. Our hotel embodies a unique aesthetic that respects and enhances its natural surroundings, with architecture that blends seamlessly with the landscape, offering sweeping views of the ocean from every vantage point .At The Seaside Siesta, we believe in providing an unparalleled guest experience. Each of our rooms and suites is thoughtfully designed to ensure comfort, privacy, and a profound sense of relaxation. From plush bedding to state-of-the-art amenities, every detail has been curated to enhance your stay. Dining at The Seaside Siesta is nothing short of a culinary journey. Our chefs specialize in a variety of cuisines, focusing on fresh, local ingredients that pay homage to the region’s rich gastronomic heritage.
